Accessibility

The venue is fully accessible and DDA Compliant.

The room we're using is steeply banked and has 2 entrances, one at the front of the room (2nd floor) and one at the back of the room (3rd floor). There are wheelchair spaces at the front of the room on the 2nd floor.
